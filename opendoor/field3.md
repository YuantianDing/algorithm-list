
## Field of science 3. Informatics and Information Systems

1. Concept of information: General characteristics of information collection, transmission, 
2. processing, and storage; information measurement. Units of information measurement. Information and entropy.
3. Concept of information systems and information technologies.
4. Hardware and software tools of information technologies.
5. Concept of a system: Systems in engineering, economics, and living nature. Types of systems. Control object and control system. Information. Feedback. Subject of technical cybernetics and information theory.
6. Modeling as a scientific method of cybernetics: Types of models. Models of technical, biological, and socio-economic systems. Concept of a "black box". Identification problem. Model adequacy.
7. Pragmatic, semantic, and syntactic aspects of information.
8. Reliability of the hardware-software complex of an information system.
